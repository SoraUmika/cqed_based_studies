# Auto Workflow Probe Study

Study Name: auto_workflow_probe

Run a minimal cQED ANA study that starts from first principles and answers this question:

Can free dispersive evolution alone generate a conditional phase difference of pi between the n=0 and n=1 cavity manifolds for a qubit over a finite idle time?

Keep the scope deliberately small:
- derive the analytic condition first,
- run only a lightweight numerical confirmation,
- generate one simple figure and one small artifact,
- keep total compute time under two minutes,
- produce the standard study outputs needed by the research loop.
